About

With over 70 years of combined experience in the Canadian textile and craft markets, we at the Cozy Corner are well-prepared to answer any questions you may have. Our wide selection of products and services is available to you through strictly Canadian born-and-bred suppliers and vendors.

The team at the Cozy Corner includes myself, Kit Marshall, in the role of President and Communications Overseer; Marty O'Connell, Vice President and Productions Manager; Kaley Hundas, as Web Developer and Merchandiser; and Peter Felhouse, Buyer and Quality Control Supervisor.

Please look around and feel free to get in touch with us if you have any questions.
